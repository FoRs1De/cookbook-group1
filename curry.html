Chickpea Curry

This chickpea curry is full of amazing flavours, with just enough spiciness. Serve it with some rice and bread.

Measurements Ingredients
1 Onion
3 gloves Garlic
1 Hot red chilli
3 cm Ginger
3 tsp Sea salt
1 tsp Ground turmeric
1 tsp Mustard seeds
1 tsp Ground coriander
1 tsp Mustard seeds
1 tsp Ground cumin
1 tsp Ground fenugreek
2 tbs Tomato puree
2 cans Chickpeas
1 can Coconut milk
