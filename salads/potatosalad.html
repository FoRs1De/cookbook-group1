Easy potato salad

This potato salad is great on the side or as a light cold lunch in the summertime.

Measurements Ingredients
2kg Potatoes
400g Mayonnaise
30ml Mustard
15ml Apple cider vinegar
4 Eggs
80g Sweet onion
15ml Dill
15ml Celery seeds
Salt and pepper
