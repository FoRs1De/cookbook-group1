Guacamole

A homemade guacamole seasoning blend is quick to make and is the perfect dip for your tortilla chips or as a spread on toast.

Measurements Ingredients
4 Ripe avocados
2 tsp Onion powder
1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
1 tsp Sea salt
1/8 tsp Black pepper
2 tbs Fresh cilantro
1/2 Large lime
